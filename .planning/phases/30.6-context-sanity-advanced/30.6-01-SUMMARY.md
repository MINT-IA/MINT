## Deviations from Plan

### Rule 1 auto-fix (D-14 pattern 2 regex asymmetry)

**Found during:** Task 1 GREEN phase (test 2 red on first run)
**Issue:** Original regex from RESEARCH §Example 1 was `/\.dart\b[^\n]*screens\//i` — only matched `.dart ... screens/` (e.g., `xxx.dart screens/yyy`). Real prompts are `apps/mobile/lib/screens/xxx.dart` (screens/ BEFORE .dart) → zero matches in practice.
**Fix:** Changed to symmetric alternation `/(?:screens\/[^\n]*\.dart\b|\.dart\b[^\n]*screens\/)/i` — catches both orderings. Sub-pattern identical aside from ordering; non-capturing group used.
**Files modified:** `.claude/hooks/mint-context-injector.js` (1 line)
**Commit:** included in `89b6fb61` (same atomic commit)
**Test delta:** test 2 went from RED to GREEN after fix; all 11 context-injector tests pass.

**Why flagged as Rule 1 (bug), not Rule 3 (architectural):** The pattern intent per D-14 is "`.dart` in `screens/` mentioned" — order-agnostic. The verbatim regex from RESEARCH was a semantic bug, not a design choice. Fix preserves D-14 intent + makes it match reality.
